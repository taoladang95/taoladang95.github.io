About Us

Welcome to GENB

We are a dedicated electrical solutions company committed to delivering safe, reliable, and high-quality services for homes and businesses. With a team of licensed electricians and experienced technicians, we provide comprehensive solutions ranging from system installation and maintenance to troubleshooting and emergency support.

Our mission is to ensure that every customer receives professional service backed by industry-standard safety practices and modern electrical technologies. Whether you need a new installation, an upgrade, or fast assistance during an unexpected outage, we are here to provide efficient and long-lasting results you can trust.

Residential Maintenance

We provide routine home electrical inspections, repair faulty outlets and switches, upgrade outdated wiring, maintain indoor/outdoor lighting, and ensure your household system operates safely and efficiently.

Commercial Maintenance

Our team maintains office and shop electrical systems, including distribution boards, lighting networks, UPS backups, security wiring, and load-balancing checks to keep your business running without interruption.

Safety Maintenance

We perform essential safety checks such as RCD/RCBO testing, grounding inspections, insulation resistance tests, thermal scanning for hotspots, and full compliance assessments to prevent electrical hazards.

Solar & Battery Maintenance

We inspect and clean solar panels, check inverter performance, evaluate battery health, verify system connectivity, and optimize overall efficiency to ensure maximum renewable energy output.
